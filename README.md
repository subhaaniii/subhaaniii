## Featured AI/ML Projects

### Spectral Geometry Embedding Analysis
Analyzes embedding spaces using neighborhood preservation, clustering behavior, graph connectivity, and spectral diagnostics to study collapse, distortion, and over-separation.

### FT-Transformer EHR Retrieval
Compares MLP and FT-Transformer-style tabular encoders for EHR-style retrieval using controlled synthetic benchmark settings.

### False-Negative-Aware Contrastive Learning
Studies the effect of false negatives in contrastive retrieval and compares standard InfoNCE with a false-negative-aware training strategy.

### Propensity Matching for Multimodal Pairs
Explores pseudo-pairing and pair-quality effects in multimodal retrieval experiments.

### CXR-Text Bridge Retrieval
Studies CXR-text retrieval and multimodal alignment in controlled medical AI-style settings.
